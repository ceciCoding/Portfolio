About    me

Hi! I’m Cecilia Olivera, Frontend Developer, UX/UI enthusiast and worshiper of cats.
In early 2019 I decided I was going to make a life-changing twist, so I embarked on the journey of software engineering and design. I had programmed when I was around 12 and the thought of it has remained at the back of my mind for all these years. I quit my job and started studying full time. I haven’t regretted it once since then.

My main resources have been Frontend Masters, Platzi, CS50, Crehana, Zero to Mastery, Javascript30 and thousands of articles, videos and forums this great community has to offer. I’m constantly craving to learn new stuff and it’s very important to me to understand things deeply ,so during this time I’ve focused on learning computer science foundations and having a good knowledge of Javascript and CSS.
Good practices and accessibility are two of my concerns as well.

Other things I enjoy are music, board games, science fiction, nerd Youtube channels, cooking and singing.

90s Music Memory Game

This is a memory game with a UI simulating a 1990s sound system. Each card is an album I like and every time there is a match a song from that album will play.
