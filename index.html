About   me

Hi! I’m Cecilia Olivera, Frontend Developer , UX/UI enthusiast and worshiper of cats.
In early 2019 I decided I was going to make a life changing twist, so I embarked on the journey of software engineering and design. I had programmed a bit when I was 12 and the thought of it remained at the back of my mind for all these years. I quit my job and started studying full time. I haven’t regretted it once since since then.
My main resources have been Frontend Masters, Platzi, CS50, Crehana, Zero to Mastery and thousands of articles, videos and forums this great community has to offer.
I’m constantly craving to learn new stuff and it’s very important to me to understand things deeply ,so during this time I’ve focused on learning computer science foundations and having a good knowledge of Javascript and CSS. Good practices and accessibility are two of my concerns as well.
My goal is to work for a company that encourages me to learn, helps me grow and is concerned about work ethics.
Other things I enjoy are music, board games, science fiction, youtube nerd channels, cooking and singing.
